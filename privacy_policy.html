Our Privacy Policy

1. Use of Service

Users must be at least 18 years of age or under the supervision of a legal guardian. All information provided during registration or booking must be accurate and up to date. Misuse of the service may lead to suspension or legal action.

2. Booking & Ride

All bookings must include correct pickup and drop-off details. The service provider is not responsible for delays, cancellations, or missed rides due to incorrect or incomplete information provided by the user.

3. Pricing

Prices are calculated based on distance, duration, and real-time demand. Peak hour or high-demand situations may include surge pricing. All applicable taxes and fees will be clearly communicated before booking confirmation.

4. Conduct

Users and drivers are expected to behave respectfully and maintain proper conduct. Any damage caused to the vehicle or harm to the driver will result in penalties and possible legal proceedings.

5. Availability

Our services are generally available 24/7; however, availability may vary based on location, vehicle availability, technical issues, or unforeseen events such as weather or road conditions.

1. Free Cancellation

Users can cancel their ride within 5 minutes of booking without any charges. If the driver has already been dispatched or arrived at the pickup point, cancellation charges may apply.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are eligible under specific conditions such as driver cancellation, duplicate charges, failed transactions, or system-related errors during booking. Refunds are not applicable for completed rides or no-shows.

3. Non-Refundable Scenarios

We do not offer refunds if the ride has been completed successfully, or if the user fails to be present at the pickup location without prior notice. Charges will apply for such instances.

4. Refund Processing

All eligible refunds will be processed within 7 to 10 business days to the original payment method. In case of any delay, users are advised to contact customer support with relevant booking details.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and transactional data including full name, email, phone number, payment method, pickup/drop location, GPS coordinates, device information, and ride history for operational purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to process bookings, enhance user experience, communicate offers/promotions, send notifications, resolve disputes, and improve the overall functionality of the platform.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. Data may be shared with drivers, payment gateways, or government/legal authorities as required by law or to ensure user safety and compliance.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement advanced security measures including SSL encryption, firewalls, and secure data storage to safeguard your personal and payment data. We regularly update our systems to protect against unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, update, delete, or withdraw consent regarding your personal data. You can contact our support team to exercise these rights as per applicable data protection laws.
